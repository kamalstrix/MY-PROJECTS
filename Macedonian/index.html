Macedonian Theatre Group (Boomerang) Melbourne,Australia Inc.

Making A Difference Theater

Macedonian Theatre Boomerang (MTG) was first formed in 1991 by a small group of theatre loving performers who are still part of the cast today. Boomerang is an amateur theatre group and is still the most successful and most popular Macedonian theatre company outside the Republic of Macedonia. Boomerang's well established links within the Macedonian community have assured its audiences of fulfilling and satisfying plays whenever Boomreang is on tour.
